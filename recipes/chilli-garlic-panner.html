Chilli Garlic Paneer

Description:

Indulge in the tantalizing fusion of fiery chilly and aromatic garlic with mouth watery paneer cubes.

Ingredients

  • 10 - 12 paneer cubes
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 to 3 tablespoon chilly sauce
  • 2 table spoon plain yogurt
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon butter

Steps:

  1. Combine garlic, chilly sauce, plain yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, salt and mix untill well combined
  2. Add the above paste to paneer cubes, tossing well to coat thoroughhly on both sides. Wrap and marinate paneer for atleast 4 hours.
  3. Melt butter in non-stick pan over medium-hiigh heat and pan-fry paneer untill well browned, about 2 minutes per side. And it's ready to serve.
